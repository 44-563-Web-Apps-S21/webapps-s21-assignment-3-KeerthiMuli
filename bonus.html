  1. The Trailhead for the Lion's Lair Trail is located along Sunshine Canyon Drive a little over a mile from the Centennial Trailhead.
  2. In Boulder, take Mapleton Ave west until it becomes Sunshine Canyon Drive.
  3. Continue west for about 1 mile and the trailhead will be on the right.
  4. The trailhead may be hard to spot. Look for the large wood steps rising from a wide private road.
  5. There is not parking along the private road but there is parking just around the corner on Sunshine Canyon Drive, enough space for about 6 cars.
  6. If this parking is full, continue west on Sunshine Canyon Drive for 1/2 mile to the access point for the Lion's Lair Spur Trail. Parking at the spur trailhead cuts about 1 mile (RT) from the hike.
