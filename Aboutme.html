APJ Abdul Kalam

Abdul Kalam is the former President of India. He is given the title of 'Missile Man of India'. A national hero, APJ Abdul Kalam became the 11th President of India in the year 2002. Life will always throw challenges to us but a true winner is the one who does not get defeated to those difficult circumstances. Keeps on finding ways to beat the challenges and fight right till success comes to us. Dr Kalam had been one such gallant fighter and his bravery to face challenges with a smile powered him to elevate to the cult status of a role model for all.
